# No-Maintenance Squash Trial - 3-Year Development Plan

## Purpose

Test squash production in scattered pits with low input and minimum intervention.

## Year 1

- establish scattered squash pits;
- record planting conditions;
- track watering and labor;
- observe survival and yield;
- document storage quality.

## Year 2

- repeat only successful pit designs;
- compare locations;
- reduce unnecessary intervention;
- improve mulch and soil cover around pits.

## Year 3

- define a repeatable low-input squash method;
- identify best locations and pit designs;
- update Crop Matrix and Plant Profiles from field results.
