# Greenhouse 2026 - 3-Year Development Plan

## Purpose

Use the greenhouse as a protected production and observation system.

## Year 1

- document current lapad and nettle dominance;
- preserve useful biomass;
- introduce selected crops carefully;
- test amaranth seed production;
- record workflow, water and labor needs.

## Year 2

- improve crop placement;
- expand only useful crops;
- increase seed and biomass production;
- connect observations to Plant Profiles and Crop Matrix.

## Year 3

- stabilize greenhouse workflow;
- refine protected cultivation methods;
- maintain only processes that justify the required work.
