# Gaitanevo 2026 - 3-Year Development Plan

## Purpose

Use the field setting for outdoor growing, crop comparison, biomass production and practical soil-building experience.

## Year 1

- document existing site conditions;
- test low-input crops;
- establish practical field routines;
- record water demand and weed pressure;
- collect useful seed where possible.

## Year 2

- expand only successful crops;
- improve mulch and compost cycles;
- compare field performance with Crop Matrix scores;
- increase efficiency of labor and watering.

## Year 3

- stabilize crop placement;
- build recurring soil-cover cycles;
- use local observations for future field planning.
