# Gaitanevo 2026

## Status

Active.

## Purpose

Gaitanevo 2026 documents outdoor growing, soil observation and field practice.

The project is used for learning, crop comparison, biomass production and practical field workflow notes.

## Feeds Data Into

- Field Journal;
- Crop Matrix;
- Plant Profiles;
- Soil Improvement Strategy;
- 3-Year Plan.

## Key Metrics

- survival;
- water demand;
- weed pressure;
- yield;
- biomass;
- labor requirement.
