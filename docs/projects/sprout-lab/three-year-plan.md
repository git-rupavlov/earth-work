# Sprout Lab - 3-Year Development Plan

## Purpose

Develop a reliable indoor system for sprouts, microgreens, seed viability testing and seedling workflow.

## Year 1

- stabilize tray workflow;
- document germination rates;
- test priority crops;
- record yield per tray;
- reduce mold and cleaning failures;
- connect observations to Crop Matrix and Field Journal.

## Year 2

- improve repeatability;
- standardize batch logs;
- compare light and airflow settings;
- expand seed viability testing;
- support seedlings for field and greenhouse projects.

## Year 3

- maintain reliable weekly production;
- use collected data to refine crop scores;
- integrate sensors and dashboards where useful;
- keep only processes that are worth repeating.
