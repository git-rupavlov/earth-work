# Balcony Pot House

## Status

Active.

## Purpose

Balcony Pot House documents container growing in apartment balcony conditions.

The focus is practical urban production, cultivar testing and observation under limited space.

## Current / Candidate Crops

- cherry tomato;
- pink tomato;
- peppers;
- beans;
- amaranth;
- dill;
- herbs and companion plants.

## Feeds Data Into

- Plant Profiles;
- Crop Matrix;
- Field Journal;
- Microclimate Lab;
- 3-Year Plan.

## Key Metrics

- container size;
- water demand;
- sun exposure;
- heat stress;
- yield;
- pruning response;
- pest pressure;
- seed collection potential.
