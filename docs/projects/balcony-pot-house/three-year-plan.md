# Balcony Pot House - 3-Year Development Plan

## Purpose

Develop a practical balcony container growing system for limited-space food production and crop observation.

## Year 1

- document current containers and crops;
- track water demand;
- observe heat stress;
- compare pruning and support methods;
- collect useful seed where realistic.

## Year 2

- improve container layout;
- standardize soil mixes;
- test resilient crops;
- compare yield per container;
- connect results to Plant Profiles and Crop Matrix.

## Year 3

- keep the best-performing crops;
- reduce wasted watering and labor;
- use the balcony as a compact cultivar test site.
