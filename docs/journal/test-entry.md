# Tomato Vegetative Start Trial 2026

## Summary

Practical comparison of two simple methods for starting new tomato plants from removed side growth.

## Context

Cultivar: Pink Dream / Розова мечта.

Date: June 2026.

Conditions:

- 07:00;
- overcast;
- about 19°C.

Mother plant notes:

- plant height after support: about 65 cm;
- about 15-20% of leaf mass removed;
- two similar plant sections used;
- section length: about 10 cm.

## Method A - Soil

One plant section was placed directly into soil immediately after removal.

About 7 cm was placed below soil level.

Upper leaves were kept.

## Method B - Water

One plant section was placed in about 4 cm of water near an indoor window.

## Observation Plan

Observation period: 14 days.

Tracked indicators:

- leaf condition;
- turgor;
- new growth;
- visible root development;
- final establishment.

## Success Criteria

A method is recorded as successful if the plant stays stable, shows new growth and continues development after day 14.

## Notes

Tomatoes can produce roots along buried stem sections. This makes removed side growth useful for practical comparison trials.
